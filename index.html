THE BAND

Tiger Bonesaw is a rock band hailing from Germany. Their sound is melodic, yet energetic and hard-hitting, often inspired by artists as diverse as Foo Fighters, Biffy Clyro, Queens Of The Stone Age, and more.

The group also holds distinctive stoner rock influences, adding depth and atmospherics to their sound due to the diverse background of the band’s line-up. The group features guitarist Carsten Przyluczky, as well as bassist Stefan Marien, lead vocalist / guitarist Dennis Hadrika, and last but not least, Jorg Skaramaga behind the drum kit.

Recently, Tiger Bonesaw set out to release their debut album, which is also accompanied by a music video for leading single “The Mean Scene.”
